The Quality Control module ensures all incoming articles meet aviation quality and certification requirements before entering the warehouse inventory.

## Overview

Quality Control provides inspection workflows for incoming articles, certificate verification, and quarantine management to maintain airworthiness compliance.

## Incoming Inspection Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Incoming Status">
    Articles arrive from purchasing with status INCOMING
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="QC Review">
    Quality inspector reviews:
    - Physical condition
    - Certificates (8130, vendor, manufacturer)
    - Part number verification
    - Serial number verification
    - Packaging and labeling
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Decision">
    Inspector decides:
    - **APPROVE**: Article passes, moves to WAITING_TO_LOCATE
    - **QUARANTINE**: Issues found, article held
    - **REJECT**: Non-conforming, return to vendor
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Format Generation">
    Approved articles require reception format before warehouse location
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Warehouse Handoff">
    Approved articles flow to warehouse for location assignment
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Article Statuses in QC

Quality Control manages articles through these statuses:

| Status | Description | QC Action |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| **INCOMING** | Just arrived, awaiting QC | Inspect and review certificates |
| **WAITING_FOR_FORMAT** | Approved, needs reception format | Generate format document |
| **WAITING_TO_LOCATE** | QC complete, ready for warehouse | Transfer to warehouse |
| **QUARANTINE** | Quality issue, under investigation | Review and disposition |

## Reception Format Generation

Approved articles need a **reception format** (receiving document):

<Accordion title="Reception Format Process">
  1. **Select Articles**: Inspector selects articles that passed QC
  2. **Generate Format**: System creates standardized receiving document with:
     - Article details
     - Inspection date and inspector
     - Certificate verification status
     - QC approval signature
  3. **Print/Save**: Format printed or saved as PDF
  4. **Status Update**: Articles move to WAITING_TO_LOCATE
  5. **Warehouse Handoff**: Format accompanies articles to warehouse
</Accordion>

## Quarantine Management

### Quarantine Process

When quality issues are identified:

1. **Quarantine Creation**:
   - Article placed in quarantine status
   - Physical segregation in quarantine area
   - Quarantine tag applied

2. **Documentation**:
   - Issue description
   - Photos of discrepancy
   - Certificate deficiencies noted

3. **Investigation**:
   - Contact vendor for resolution
   - Request corrected certificates
   - Determine if part is serviceable

4. **Disposition**:
   - **Release**: Issue resolved, approve to warehouse
   - **Return to Vendor**: Non-conforming, initiate RMA
   - **Scrap**: Beyond repair, dispose properly

### Quarantine Reasons

Common quarantine triggers:

- Missing or invalid certificates
- Part number mismatch
- Serial number discrepancy
- Physical damage
- Suspect unapproved parts
- Expired shelf-life items
- Incorrect packaging

## Inspection Checklist

<Accordion title="Standard Inspection Points">
  **Visual Inspection**:
  - ✓ Part number matches PO
  - ✓ Serial number recorded (if applicable)
  - ✓ No visible damage
  - ✓ Proper packaging
  - ✓ Labels legible and correct
  
  **Documentation Review**:
  - ✓ FAA 8130 present and valid
  - ✓ Certificate part number matches article
  - ✓ Certificate serial number matches (if serialized)
  - ✓ Certificate not expired
  - ✓ Vendor certificate matches PO
  
  **Quantity Verification**:
  - ✓ Quantity received matches PO
  - ✓ Unit of measure correct
  - ✓ No shortages or overages
  
  **Condition Verification**:
  - ✓ Condition code appropriate (SV, OH, etc.)
  - ✓ Shelf-life items within date (if applicable)
  - ✓ No signs of counterfeit
</Accordion>

## Certificate Management

QC maintains digital copies of certificates:

- **Upload**: Scan certificates and attach to article record
- **Storage**: Certificates stored with article in system
- **Retrieval**: Accessible for audits and traceability
- **Retention**: Maintained per regulatory requirements

## Integration with Warehouse

Seamless handoff to warehouse:

<Note>
**Status Flow**:

INCOMING (QC) → WAITING_FOR_FORMAT (QC) → WAITING_TO_LOCATE (Warehouse) → LOCATED (Warehouse) → AVAILABLE (Inventory)

Only QC-approved articles reach warehouse for location.
</Note>

## Best Practices

<Note>
**QC Best Practices**:
- Inspect articles promptly upon receipt
- Never approve articles without proper certificates
- Photograph any discrepancies
- Maintain quarantine area security
- Document all inspection decisions
- Escalate suspect unapproved parts immediately
- Retain certificates with article records
- Conduct periodic self-audits
</Note>
