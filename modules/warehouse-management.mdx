The Warehouse Management module provides comprehensive inventory control for aviation parts, tools, and consumables. It handles the complete lifecycle from receiving to dispatch.

## Article Types

The system manages different article classifications:

### Aeronautical Articles
- **Component Articles**: Aircraft parts with TSN/TSO tracking, hour/cycle limits
- **Consumable Articles**: Expendable items with quantity and expiration management
- **Tool Articles**: Special tools requiring calibration and maintenance

### General Articles
- Office supplies and consumables
- Equipment and materials not aircraft-specific

## Warehouse Operations

### Incoming Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="In Transit">
    Articles are marked as in transit when purchase orders are confirmed with tracking information (USA tracking, OCK tracking)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Incoming Reception">
    When articles arrive, they enter the incoming status for initial inspection and documentation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Quality Control">
    Articles flow to Quality Control module for certification review (8130, vendor certificates)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Waiting to Locate">
    Approved articles await physical location assignment in the warehouse
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Located & Available">
    Articles are assigned to warehouse zones and become available for dispatch
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Dispatch Workflow

<Accordion title="Dispatch Request Process">
  1. **Request Creation**: Users create dispatch requests specifying:
     - Part number and quantity needed
     - Destination (work order, department, or general use)
     - Justification and urgency
  
  2. **Warehouse Processing**: Warehouse staff:
     - Locate the requested articles by batch and serial
     - Verify condition and certificates
     - Prepare items for dispatch
  
  3. **Dispatch Confirmation**: 
     - Articles are removed from inventory
     - Dispatch documentation is generated
     - Receiving party confirms receipt
</Accordion>

### Tool Management

<Accordion title="Toolbox System">
  **Toolboxes** are assigned to employees (typically maintenance technicians) and contain:
  
  - Tool inventory with serial numbers
  - Calibration due dates for precision tools
  - Special tool flags for critical equipment
  - Delivery and return tracking
  
  **Dashboard Alerts**:
  - Tools requiring calibration
  - Overdue tool returns
  - Expired calibration certificates
</Accordion>

## Inventory Management

### Batch System

Articles are organized into **batches** for efficient management:

```typescript
Batch Properties:
- Name and description
- ATA code (aircraft system classification)
- Category (e.g., "FASTENERS", "AVIONICS", "ENGINE PARTS")
- Brand/manufacturer
- Hazardous material flag
- Primary unit of measure
- Minimum quantity threshold
- Warehouse zone assignment
```

### Quantity Tracking

<Note>
The system supports multiple units of measure with automatic conversion:
- Primary unit (e.g., "EACH", "BOX")
- Secondary units with equivalence ratios
- Automatic conversion in dispatch and incoming operations
</Note>

### Stock Alerts

The warehouse dashboard provides real-time visibility:

- **Out of Stock Articles**: Items below minimum quantity
- **Restock Needed**: Low inventory alerts based on batch minimum
- **Tool Calibration**: Tools approaching or past calibration due dates
- **User Statistics**: Dispatch and incoming activity by warehouse staff

## Article Conditions

Aviation articles are tracked by regulatory condition:

| Condition | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **SV (Serviceable)** | New or overhauled, airworthy with certificates |
| **OH (Overhauled)** | Completely overhauled to manufacturer specs |
| **RP (Repairable)** | Requires repair before use |
| **AR (As Removed)** | Removed from aircraft, condition unknown |
| **BER (Beyond Economic Repair)** | Not economically repairable |
| **INSP (Inspection)** | Requires inspection before release |

## Warehouse Zones

Physical organization by location and type:

- Zone assignment for efficient picking
- Location-based inventory searches
- Multi-warehouse support for different bases

## User Workflows

### For Warehouse Staff

1. **Daily Operations**:
   - Review pending dispatch requests
   - Process incoming articles
   - Locate and assign warehouse zones
   - Update inventory quantities

2. **Inventory Control**:
   - Conduct cycle counts
   - Manage tool calibrations
   - Monitor stock levels
   - Generate inventory reports

### For Requesters

1. **Create dispatch requests** for needed parts
2. **Track request status** through the workflow
3. **Confirm receipt** of dispatched items
4. **View inventory availability** before requesting
